CipherOS is a custom Android operating system based on AOSP and fueled by LineageOS. We strive to provide our users with a clean, bloat free and sturdy experience with minimal customisation.

Open Source

Don’t see a feature you want? Have experience with custom roms? Fork it from Github and make it your own!

Up to Date

We constantly merge the latest security patches provided by Google to insure your device is as secure as possible.

Device Support

We support a wide range of devices. More are coming soon!
